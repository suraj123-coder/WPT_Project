About Us

Welcome to CDAC Mumbai College Library

The CDAC Mumbai College Library is a state-of-the-art facility dedicated to providing resources and support to the students, faculty, and staff of CDAC Mumbai. With a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources, we strive to meet the academic and research needs of our community.

Library Mission

Our mission is to support the educational and research goals of CDAC Mumbai by providing access to high-quality information resources and promoting effective use of information and technology.
